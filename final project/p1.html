Polaroid SX-70

$120

The Polaroid SX-70 is a classic instant camera with a foldable design and an aluminum body. It features a fixed 116mm lens with adjustable aperture settings and an automatic exposure system that adjusts the shutter speed based on lighting conditions. The camera uses SX-70 instant film, which produces square prints that develop within minutes. Notable for its reflex mirror system, the SX-70 offers precise framing through the viewfinder and includes a self-timer for delayed shots.
